Who I Am

I am a web developer with an engineering degree and a master's degree in AI & Data Science. Programming is my passion, and I find the most satisfaction in creating applications that combine modern web technologies with elements of artificial intelligence and data analysis. I develop my skills through both academic and private projects, always eager to explore new technologies and approaches, adapting them flexibly to the needs of each project.

What I Do

I build applications from scratch – from refined interfaces and user-friendly experiences to backend services with my own API and business logic. I’ve completed many experimental and academic projects – the most interesting ones are presented here. How I Like to Work

I value open communication, constructive feedback, and a positive atmosphere that encourages sharing knowledge and experience. I enjoy collaborating within a team, but I am equally comfortable working independently. I care about continuous growth, expanding my skills, and bringing real value to projects, especially those built with modern technologies.
